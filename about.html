What do we do

Make your customers happy by giving services.

Tectonic Engineering is a highly professional Engineering Consultancy firm. Founded in 2015 as a humble practice, started by first generation professionals, ‘TE’ has proudly delivered across INDIA and has since transformed into a multidisciplinary firm, known for its creative, innovative analysis & design approach. The organisation renders services in Structural Design & Detailing, Estimation & Costing, MEP & Fire Service, Solar System, & BIM applications. Focussed on sustainable designs.

‘TE’ has been providing services for many projects including government and private clients, Public Works Departments, Development Authorities, and Public Sector Undertakings of the State & Central Governments in India. Our skilled team ensure high quality service for our partners,
